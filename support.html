    Why Donate to Lokarpan ?

    We are facing our worst inequity ever. Millions of children in India do not access to quality education. We envision a future where each one of these children will have an opportunity to attain an excellent education. It is this belief that every child can and must attain an excellent education, that drives each one of us at Lokarpan to work harder. We do all that we can to support every Fellow with training, mentors and resources to help them put children on a di erent life path.
